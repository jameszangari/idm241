ORIGINAL INTERFACE

James Cliff [Personal Website] — I will be adding a hamburger menu for quick and easy navigation of my site.


TRIGGERS

  • The first trigger will occur when the user hovers over the 3-lined hamburger icon ( ☰ ) in the top left corner of the website (to be added).
  • Another trigger will occur after clicking the ☰ icon.
  • An additional trigger will occur when the user hovers over one of the navigtion menu items.
  • Another trigger will occur after clicking the one of the navigation menu items.

    • RULES

  • On hover, the hamburger icon will slightly change color.
  • On click, the hamburger icon ☰ will transform into an X with the middle line fading out, while the top and bottom lines converge into the X shape.
  • After clicking the hamburger icon, the navigation menu will slide in from the left of the screen and push the page content to the right.

    • In this navigation menu will be 6 menu items: About Me, Portfolio, Digital Media, Short Films, Photography, and Contact. Clicking one of the menu items will close the menu and jump to that section in the webpage.

    • Hovering over any of these navigation items will change the color to a blue hover state color.
    • During the click one of these menu items, the text will scale up 30% and return back to its orignal state after the mouse click.
